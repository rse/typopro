Derivation: Russian Sans Serif Types

PT Sans is based on Russian sans serif types of the second part of
the XX century, but at the same time has a very distinctive features
of modern humanistic design. The family consists of 8 styles: 4 basic
styles; 2 captions styles for small sizes and 2 narrows styles for
economic setting.
